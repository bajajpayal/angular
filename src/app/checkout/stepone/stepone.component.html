Stylist Experience

Our pricing varies based on the stylist's level of experience. We strive to provide options that can fit into a variety of budgets.

Pretty Stylists 1 - 3 years of experience

Pretty stylists are recent graduates from the most respected cosmetology schools. We recommend booking blowouts and makeup touch-ups with these stylists.

Beautiful Stylists 3 - 6 years of experience

Beautiful stylists have refined their craft over many years with a wide range of clientele. We recommend beautiful stylists for date nights, corporate events, black tie affairs and proms.

Glamorous Stylists Over 6 years of experience, published work.

Glamorous stylists have extensive experience, published work, and high profile and/or celebrity clientele. We recommend glamorous stylists for brides, red carpet events, camera-ready occasions, or if you just want to enjoy the full VIP experience.
